## ðŸ’¡ Key Insights for AI Agent

### Critical Design Parameters

**Tempo Guidelines**:
- Lullaby/Sleep: 60-80 BPM
- Calming: 70-90 BPM
- Learning: 90-120 BPM
- Energizing: 120-160 BPM

**Age-Based Complexity**:
- 0-2 years: 80-90% repetition
- 2-3 years: 60-70% repetition
- 3-4 years: 40-50% repetition
- 4-6 years: 30-40% repetition

**Vocabulary Levels**:
- 0-2 years: 50-300 words
- 2-3 years: 300-1,000 words
- 3-4 years: 1,000-2,000 words
- 4-6 years: 2,000-5,000 words

**Attention Span = Song Length**:
- 0-2 years: 2-3 minutes
- 2-3 years: 2.5-3.5 minutes
- 3-4 years: 3-4 minutes
- 4-6 years: 3-6 minutes

**Vocal Range**:
- Children: D4 to D5 (middle C to D above)
- Stay within this range for singability

**Safety Limits**:
- Maximum volume: 85 decibels
- Lullabies: 40-50 decibels
- Active songs: 60-70 decibels
